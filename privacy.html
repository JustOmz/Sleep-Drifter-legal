Last updated: 2026-01-06

Summary

Sleep Drifter is designed to work offline. We do not require accounts or logins, and the core features work without you sharing personal information.

Information stored on your device

Sleep Drifter stores some settings locally on your device to make the app work the way you expect. This may include:

  • Timer preferences and fade-out settings
  • Favourites
  • Last selected sound and volume preference

This information stays on your device and is not uploaded by Sleep Drifter.

In-app purchases

If you purchase “Lifetime Unlock”, purchase information is processed to validate your purchase and enable premium features, and to allow you to restore purchases on your devices.

RevenueCat (purchase management)

We use RevenueCat to manage in-app purchases and entitlement status (for example, confirming that “Lifetime Unlock” is active). RevenueCat processes purchase-related information for this purpose. This may include purchase history and an app-specific identifier used to associate entitlements with your device/app install.

RevenueCat’s privacy policy: https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy/

Data sharing

We do not sell your personal data. Purchase-related information may be processed by RevenueCat solely to provide purchase and restore functionality.

Support

If you contact us by email, we will receive the information you include in your message. We use it only to respond to your request.

Your choices

  • You can delete the app to remove locally stored preferences from your device.
  • You can restore “Lifetime Unlock” from within the app using “Restore Purchases”.

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at omeral i1995@hotmail.co.uk.
